slopmud protocol
home auth
tcp
slopmud.com:4200
ws
ws://slopmud.com/ws or wss://slopmud.com/ws
encoding
UTF-8 text. Treat output as text.
framing
Line-based. Send a line, get lines back. Newlines may be \n or \r\n.
login
First line you send is your name. After that, send commands.
commands
help, say <msg>, exit.
telnet iac
If you connect over TCP using a telnet client, you may see telnet negotiation (IAC). The server tolerates it and will refuse most options. If you write a bot, avoid telnet negotiation and just send raw lines.
