# Gaia Areas (Summary, Draft)

This is an internal summary of areas and how they fit together. It is not meant to be player-facing, and can include "secret sauce" like hidden exits and drop themes.

Room budgets and placeholder future areas are tracked in `docs/room_scale_plan.md`.

## Starter Region (Levels 1-5)

### Newbie School (L1-L2)

- Theme: onboarding campus; sterile halls + simulated hazards.
- Purpose: teach verbs, combat, death/recovery, inventory, grouping.
- Notable NPCs:
  - Instructor Kline (human)
  - Tutor-Unit R0 ("Rho") (robot)
- Secrets:
  - hidden supply closet with low-value consumables (teaches searching)

Sub-areas:

- Orientation Wing (L1)
- Combat Labs (L1-L2)
- Sim Yard (L2)
- Dorm Annex (L1) (social + tutorial quests)

### Town: Gaia Gate (L1-L3)

- Theme: mixed human/robot settlement; stable power and water; optimistic propaganda.
- Purpose: hub services (shop/bank/mail), social, factions, quest distribution.
- Services:
  - Market Row (buy/sell/repair)
  - Bank/Locker (anti-theft + persistence)
  - Job Board (repeatables, dailies)
  - Clinic (cheap revive, status cures)
- Secrets:
  - black-market stall (fence) behind a "maintenance door"

### Hunting Grounds: Meadowline + Scrap Orchard (L2-L3)

- Theme: pastoral edge + salvage groves (old tech half-buried in roots).
- Purpose: safe grind loop with clear geography, predictable spawns.
- Mobs:
  - meadow pests (low damage, high count)
  - scavenger drones (ranged; drop "scrap")
- Drops:
  - basic consumables, low-tier mods, crafting junk

### Under-Town Sewers (L3-L4)

- Theme: wet tunnels, valves, gas pockets, maintenance drones gone feral.
- Purpose: first dungeon loop; keys/valves, backtracking, status effects.
- Mobs:
  - slickrats, pipe leeches
  - maintenance drone (elite)
- Boss:
  - The Grease King (adds + ground hazard)
- Secrets:
  - 1 hidden bypass to the Quarry foothills (one-way early; two-way later)

### Quarry + Old Road Checkpoint (L4-L5)

- Theme: industrial edge; ore extraction; bandit salvage gangs.
- Purpose: first named elite, heavier hits, travel gate to midgame.
- Mobs:
  - quarry toughs (melee)
  - rock-biter worms (ambush)
  - scrap bandits (nets/bleeds)
- Elite:
  - Breaker-7 (telegraphed slam; teaches interrupts)
- Secrets:
  - timed "night shift" event (better ore, harder spawns)

### Hillfort Ruins (L4-L7)

- Theme: a crumbled fort with ward pylons and banner halls; half-occupied by patrols and scavengers.
- Purpose: transitional outdoor dungeon; teaches patrol timing and route selection.
- Notable:
  - Command bunker (safehouse unlock later)
  - 3 ward pylons (route objectives)
- Secrets:
  - a hidden rest niche that becomes a proper safehouse after the Hillfort storyline

## Midgame (Levels 5-10)

### Rail Spur (L5-L6)

- Theme: half-working rail line; moving event nodes.
- Purpose: travel unlock; introduce world events.
- Events:
  - supply run
  - train ambush
  - runaway cargo bot

### Rustwood (L5-L7)

- Theme: forest with rusted pylons; wildlife + stealth predators.
- Purpose: pathing/navigation; mixed pack composition.
- Mobs:
  - stump-stalkers (stealth)
  - screech hawks (pullers)
  - feral service dogs (fast)

### Sunken Library (L6-L8)

- Theme: flooded archive; knowledge as a key.
- Purpose: puzzle-ish dungeon; unlock gates via collected plates.
- Mobs:
  - mold sprites (debuffs)
  - archive sentries (ranged)
- Reward:
  - map fragments + "index key" (future shard travel hook)

### Factory District (Outskirts) (L7-L9)

- Theme: tight urban-industrial rooms; conveyors; line-of-sight.
- Purpose: tactical combat; interrupts; environmental hazards.
- Mobs:
  - foreman bruisers
  - belt-runner drones
  - weldspray turrets

### The Reservoir (L8-L10)

- Theme: water + power control; pumping stations.
- Purpose: faction conflict; resource mechanics.
- Mobs:
  - sump eels
  - pump guardians
- Boss:
  - The Flow Regulator (multi-phase)

### The Underrail (L9-L12)

- Theme: a buried transit spine; platforms, switchyards, old signal towers.
- Purpose: deep traversal dungeon; unlocks higher-tier travel (Skybridge).
- Mobs:
  - signal wights (debuffs)
  - rail sentries (ranged)
  - pack crawlers (fast)
- Boss:
  - Platform Warden (adds + terrain hazards)

## High / Late (Levels 11-20)

### Skybridge (L11-L12)

- Theme: high spans; wind; vertigo; long sightlines.
- Purpose: traversal pressure; knockback; ranged play.

### Glass Wastes (L12-L14)

- Theme: heat haze, glassed ground, storms.
- Purpose: long loops; navigation; attrition.

### Crater Gardens (L13-L16)

- Theme: reclaimed biome; engineered plants over ancient machinery.
- Purpose: mid-late wilderness with elite loops; unlocks access to The Core.
- Mobs:
  - vine striders (reach attacks)
  - bloom mites (swarms)
  - caretaker drones (heals/cleanses)
- Boss:
  - Root Engine (multi-stage)

### The Core (L15-L16)

- Theme: ancient machine heart.
- Purpose: coordinated dungeon with multi-stage bosses.

### The Seam (L17-L20)

- Theme: shard boundary; reality glitches.
- Purpose: endgame loops; shard travel; rare drops.

## Design Rules (so areas stay coherent)

- Every area has:
  - 1 safe loop (consistent XP)
  - 1 spike (elite/boss) with a distinct lesson
  - 1 secret (hidden exit, rare, lore, or shortcut)
- Every bracket has at least:
  - 2 grind loops
  - 1 dungeon-ish loop
  - 1 travel unlock
