## What They Did (Timeline)

1. Hook: the outpost wants a route opened through the wastes. The storm eye is the only window. Get in, do the thing, get out.
2. Prep: they buy water and "heat cloth" equivalents, agree on a rule: "never fight in the open during a storm pulse."
3. Dunes: long traversal with ranged pressure. Ranger picks threats, rogue scouts cover pockets, fighter keeps the party cohesive.
4. Glassfields: navigation hazard. Wizard becomes the map tool; cleric handles attrition debuffs.
5. Secret: they find a half-buried shelter tube that lets them skip a nasty open segment (feels like survival skill reward).
6. Storm eye approach: visibility drops. They learn to move on "lull windows" and stop in shelters.
7. Setpiece: storm eye event. They must stabilize a beacon/marker while waves arrive in a fixed cadence.
8. Boss-like spike: the storm manifests as a phase shift; adds + hazard. They wipe once, then win by prioritizing adds and respecting the storm rhythm.
9. Resolution: they return with a stamped route token; outpost opens the next gate.
